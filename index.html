💡 Study Tips for Success
🎧
Listen Multiple Times: Don't worry if you don't understand everything the first time. Listen again and focus on different details each time.
📝
Take Notes: Write down new words or phrases you hear. Look them up later to expand your vocabulary.
🎯
Practice Daily: Even 15-20 minutes of daily practice will improve your English faster than long, infrequent sessions.
🏆
Celebrate Progress: Collect all the badges and aim for perfect scores. Every small improvement counts!
